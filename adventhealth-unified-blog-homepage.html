Unified Blog Strategy + Homepage Redesign

Consolidation and cleanup of blog architecture: taxonomy simplification, content governance, and redesign collaboration to improve usability and performance.

Role

  • Owned blog strategy inputs across teams (governance, taxonomy, and editorial alignment).
  • Co-led consolidation planning and stakeholder alignment for migration readiness.
  • Partnered with web/CMS and creative teams on homepage redesign requirements.

Approach

  • Audited existing blog structure to identify duplication and taxonomy bloat.
  • Defined a simplified taxonomy and tagging approach for scalability and SEO clarity.
  • Aligned homepage modules and UX considerations to highlight priority stories and service lines.

Results

  • Clearer information architecture and improved navigation for readers.
  • More consistent categorization for reporting, search, and editorial planning.
  • A foundation for ongoing optimization and content performance iteration.
