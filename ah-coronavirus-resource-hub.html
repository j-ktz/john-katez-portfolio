Role

Led content strategy for a public health hub, coordinating rapid updates with clinical and brand stakeholders.

Strategy

  • Structured hub navigation around the top patient questions and care pathways.
  • Defined update cadence, review steps, and owners to keep information accurate.
  • Aligned social and site updates so audiences always landed on the same source of truth.

Outcomes

  • Centralized updates in one trusted hub for communities and patients.
  • Reduced duplicate messaging by anchoring channels to the hub.
