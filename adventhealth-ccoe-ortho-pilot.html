Content Center of Excellence — Ortho Pilot

Performance and adoption story for a content pilot: metrics visibility, asset tagging, and repeatable workflows across markets.

Role

  • Led cross-functional content operations for an enterprise pilot (strategy, governance, and enablement).
  • Defined asset tagging standards and launch documentation to support consistent adoption.
  • Partnered with stakeholders to align distribution, measurement, and reporting.

Approach

  • Built a lightweight intake → production → publish workflow with clear ownership and SLAs.
  • Implemented tagging/taxonomy guidance to improve findability and reporting fidelity.
  • Created repeatable templates and training moments to scale the pilot across markets.

Results

  • Improved visibility into performance metrics to support faster iteration.
  • Increased adoption of standardized tagging and content processes across participating markets.
  • Established a reusable playbook for future service-line pilots.
