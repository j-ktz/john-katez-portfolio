100 Whole Lives + Toolkits + Observances

Campaign planning and enterprise toolkits supporting storytelling, observance moments, and consistent brand execution across channels.

Role

  • Led planning and governance for system-wide social and content programs.
  • Developed toolkits for partners to execute consistent, on-brand moments.
  • Supported storytelling initiatives that amplified mission and patient experience.

Approach

  • Built modular toolkit components (copy, creative guidance, posting cadence, measurement).
  • Created observance calendars and content recommendations for partners to localize.
  • Aligned leaders and teams on narrative arcs, audience intent, and platform best practices.

Results

  • More consistent execution across markets and partners.
  • Faster time-to-publish for observance moments with ready-to-use assets and copy.
  • Improved stakeholder experience through clear guidance and repeatable formats.
