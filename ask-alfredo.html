#AskAlfredo — Olive Garden

Prompt-led Q&A format that turned dating and relationship questions into a repeatable, high-engagement social moment.

← Back Email LinkedIn

Role

Social lead (concept, copy patterns, community engagement, optimization).

Strategy

  • Designed a reply-first mechanic optimized for conversation velocity.
  • Codified tone-of-voice patterns to keep responses playful and brand-safe.
  • Maintained momentum via fast-turn community engagement.

Outcomes

  • Earned coverage highlighting the concept and brand voice.
  • Strong participation driven by a simple, repeatable format.
