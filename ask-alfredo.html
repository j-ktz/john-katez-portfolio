#AskAlfredo — Olive Garden

Turn dating/relationship questions into a repeatable social format that drives replies and strengthens brand affinity.

My role

Social lead (concept + copy patterns + community engagement + optimization).

Strategy

  • Built a prompt-led, reply-first format designed for conversation velocity.
  • Codified tone-of-voice patterns so responses stayed playful and brand-safe.
  • Optimized the flow for ongoing participation (easy for users to join at any time).
  • Kept momentum through quick-turn engagement and thoughtful comment management.

Outcomes

  • High reply volume and repeat participation (format proved repeatable).
  • Earned attention in marketing/entertainment coverage for the concept and voice.
