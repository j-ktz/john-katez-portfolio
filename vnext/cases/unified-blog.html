Executive Summary

The Unified Blog Ecosystem replaced fragmented workflows with a single editorial engine. By aligning audience insights, service-line priorities, and medical review into a consistent model, long-form content became the nucleus for SEO visibility and social distribution.

Performance lift followed: organic engagements rose +81%, organic saves rose +82%, and share growth scaled into the thousands of percent from a low base.

Goal

Transform siloed publishing into a scalable, SEO-informed editorial system.

Insight

Consistency in governance and structure unlocks discoverability and speed.

System

Centralized insights, review workflows, and a distribution playbook.

What I Led

  • Aligned SEO themes with editorial priorities and service-line goals.
  • Standardized long-form packaging to improve scannability and conversion.
  • Built distribution workflows that connected web, email, and social.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Partnered with digital, SEO, and medical review teams.
  • Led governance and training for consistent editorial quality.
  • Created visibility into publishing cadence and performance reporting.

Outcomes

Stronger domain authority, faster publishing velocity, and cross-platform lift as long-form stories powered social and email distribution.

Signals of Success

  • Blog of the Year recognition for enterprise editorial excellence.
  • Organic engagements +81% and organic saves +82%.
  • Share growth scaled into the thousands of percent from a low base.
