Executive Summary

The Enterprise Social Strategy aligned brand storytelling, recruitment, and community engagement into a single system. With clear platform roles and governance, teams could move faster while staying on-brand and performance-focused.

Systemwide performance exceeded 10M monthly impressions and 156K engagements, with Reels reaching a 4.8% ER.

Goal

Unify social strategy across enterprise and market-level teams.

Insight

Shared roles and benchmarks improve consistency and performance.

System

Governance standards, creative guardrails, and reporting model.

What I Led

  • Built an enterprise governance framework with approvals and escalation paths.
  • Standardized intake, briefing, and reporting across markets.
  • Developed playbooks for brand voice, community management, and issue response.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Aligned Corporate Comms, Talent Strategy, Brand Center, and Analytics.
  • Trained contributors on platform best practices and policy compliance.
  • Served as a connective hub between enterprise and regional teams.

Outcomes

Stronger alignment, improved brand safety, and measurable engagement gains across the enterprise footprint.

Signals of Success

  • Monthly impressions exceeded 10M with 156K+ engagements.
  • Reels achieved a 4.8% ER; theme-driven content reached up to 6.5% ER.
  • X/Twitter content spikes surpassed 11% ER during key moments.
