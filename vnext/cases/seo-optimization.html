Role

Executive Summary

The SEO optimization initiative strengthened the enterprise’s organic footprint by standardizing metadata, implementing schema markup, and unifying content into structured topic clusters. This improved authority, accelerated indexation, and boosted non-branded visibility.

Goal

Increase organic visibility and accelerate discovery across priority categories.

Insight

Consistent structure and E-E-A-T signals are foundational to SEO trust and ranking.

System

Metadata standards, schema deployment, and internal linking upgrades.

What I Led

  • Aligned SEO themes with editorial priorities and service-line goals.
  • Standardized metadata and schema for enterprise consistency.
  • Created topic clusters to improve authority and cross-page discovery.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Partnered with digital experience and analytics teams for search visibility.
  • Aligned social + search to amplify high-performing content.
  • Built reporting cadence to track ranking lift and engagement gains.

Outcomes

Optimized pages saw double-digit ranking lift within 60 days, while organic engagement grew +80% YoY and impressions across priority categories increased +12%.

Signals of Success

  • Double-digit ranking lift within 60 days on optimized content.
  • +80% YoY organic engagement growth tied to long-form content.
  • +12% impressions across priority categories after optimization.
