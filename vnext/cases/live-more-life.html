Executive Summary

“Live More Life” established the enterprise’s most cohesive brand architecture to date. Anchored in the “100 Whole Lives” narrative, the platform reframed care around human possibility, then scaled that message across paid, owned, and local-market channels with a unified activation playbook.

Platform-aligned storytelling delivered standout engagement: Instagram faith and whole-person content exceeded 6.5% ER, Facebook gratitude narratives performed above 2.6% ER, and LinkedIn leadership stories peaked at 7.1% ER.

Goal

Unify enterprise brand storytelling while giving markets a clear, flexible activation system.

Insight

Emotional resonance and human-centered narratives drive stronger message recall and consistency.

System

Message maps, creative standards, and activation guidance that scales across teams.

What I Led

  • Defined the brand platform narrative and message architecture.
  • Built a scalable activation playbook for paid, owned, and local teams.
  • Aligned cross-functional partners around shared story and standards.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Partnered across brand, comms, digital experience, and market teams.
  • Supported cross-team governance to maintain consistency at scale.
  • Enabled enterprise storytelling that markets could localize without losing brand integrity.

Outcomes

Systemwide branded storytelling reached 10.1M impressions in a single month while delivering higher engagement rates across priority themes.

Signals of Success

  • Instagram ER topped 6.5% for faith and whole-person storytelling.
  • Facebook gratitude narratives consistently performed above 2.6% ER.
  • LinkedIn leadership stories peaked at 7.1% ER.
