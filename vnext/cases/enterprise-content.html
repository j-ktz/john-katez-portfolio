Role

Enterprise content strategy lead

Scope

Corporate, regional, and service-line teams

Focus

Governance, taxonomy, performance standards

Executive Summary

The Enterprise Content Strategy modernized how content is planned, created, and measured across the organization. Built on clear pillars and governance, the system strengthened quality, accelerated workflows, and aligned performance standards across teams.

Systemwide reporting showed 230K+ monthly video views and 156K+ monthly engagements, with pillar ER peaking at 7%+ for whole-person care stories.

Goal

Create shared standards that drive consistency, speed, and measurable outcomes.

Insight

Clear governance and taxonomy reduce friction and improve cross-team alignment.

System

Content pillars, templates, QA checkpoints, and unified reporting.

What I Led

  • Defined enterprise content pillars and governance standards.
  • Implemented intake, briefing, and QA checkpoints to improve quality.
  • Built training and enablement for stakeholders across teams.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Partnered with legal, compliance, PR, HR, and brand teams.
  • Aligned content priorities with service-line and market goals.
  • Created repeatable workflows that reduced last-minute rework.

Outcomes

Enterprise teams gained a clear, scalable framework that strengthened consistency, compliance alignment, and content quality.

Signals of Success

  • 230K+ video views and 156K+ engagements in monthly systemwide snapshots.
  • Gratitude content delivered 4-7% ER; wellness content 1.7-4% ER.
  • Whole-person care storytelling exceeded 7% ER.
