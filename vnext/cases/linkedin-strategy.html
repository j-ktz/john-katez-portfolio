Executive-level LinkedIn strategy + reporting cadence

Executive Summary

The LinkedIn strategy reset shifted the channel from passive employer brand content to a national influence engine. The strategy introduced new formats, optimized cadence, and governance standards that elevated executive visibility and measurable engagement.

Average engagement rate climbed to 8.71%, while clicks and engagement volume surged, proving the platform could drive real action.

Goal

Increase engagement quality and drive meaningful action from a professional audience.

Insight

Narrative design + platform-native formats would lift both engagement and clicks.

System

New content formats, governance model, and analytics-driven cadence.

What I Led

  • Defined the LinkedIn playbook and performance benchmarks.
  • Aligned stakeholders on cadence, narrative priorities, and executive visibility.
  • Built dashboards and reporting cadence for leadership visibility.

Leadership + Collaboration

  • Partnered with Talent, Comms, and Digital Marketing to align goals.
  • Standardized approvals and briefing workflows for speed and clarity.
  • Coached teams on platform best practices and content packaging.

Outcomes

Engagement rate nearly doubled, post clicks surged, and follower growth accelerated—proving the strategy shift improved both quality and action.

Performance Highlights

  • ER lifted from 4.44% to 8.71% after the strategy reset.
  • Post clicks rose +235%, total engagements +159%.
  • Followers grew +12.7% in 2024.
